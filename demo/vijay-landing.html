THALAPATHY VIJAY

The Icon of Tamil Cinema

About the Legend

Real Name: Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar

Born: June 22, 1974

Birthplace: Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Occupation: Actor, Dancer, Singer, Politician

Years Active: 1984 - Present

Nickname: Thalapathy (Commander)

Vijay, fondly known as Thalapathy by millions of fans, is one of the most celebrated actors in Indian cinema. Starting as a child artist in 1984, he has starred in over 65 films. His ability to blend mass entertainment with social messages has made him a beloved figure across generations. In 2024, he founded the political party Tamizhaga Vettri Kazhagam.
