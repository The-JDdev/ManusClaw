THALAPATHY VIJAY

The Icon of Tamil Cinema

About the Legend

Real Name: Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar

Born: June 22, 1974

Birthplace: Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India

Occupation: Actor, Dancer, Playback Singer, Politician

Years Active: 1984 - Present

Nickname: Thalapathy (Commander)

Vijay, fondly known as Thalapathy (Commander) by his millions of fans, is one of the most celebrated actors in Indian cinema. Starting his career as a child artist in 1984, he has starred in over 65 films and has established himself as a box office powerhouse. His ability to blend mass entertainment with social messages has made him a beloved figure across generations. Beyond cinema, Vijay founded the political party Tamizhaga Vettri Kazhagam in 2024, marking a new chapter in his journey as a leader of the people.
